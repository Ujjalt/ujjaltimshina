  <strong> Career Aspects of Mathematics</strong>, delivered at <strong>Government Senior Secondary Schools, Daramdin and Tashiding</strong>, Sikkim, India, on the occasion of National Mathematics Day 2024, under the Department of Science and Technology, Government of Sikkim.
  
  <br><br>
  <strong>Overview:</strong> The talks were part of a state-wide outreach initiative aimed at encouraging high school students to explore mathematics beyond the classroom. I discussed the practical value, beauty, and broad career opportunities available in mathematical sciences.
  
  <br><br>
  <strong>Highlights:</strong>
  <ul>
    <li>Inspiring young students to pursue mathematics as a career</li>
    <li>Introducing key career paths: academia, data science, finance, teaching, and research</li>
    <li>Emphasizing mathematical thinking, problem-solving, and curiosity</li>
    <li>Interaction and Q&A with students on study strategies and goals</li>
  </ul>
