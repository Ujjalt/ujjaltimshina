  <strong> Career Aspects in Mathematics</strong>, delivered at <strong>Sikkim Government College, Rhenock</strong>, Rhenock, Sikkim, India, in 2024. Organized under the Department of Science and Technology, Government of Sikkim.
  
  <br><br>
  <strong>Overview:</strong> The talk aimed to inspire undergraduate students by highlighting the diverse academic and professional paths available in mathematics. Real-life examples and personal experiences were used to illustrate the scope and beauty of mathematical thinking.
  
  <br><br>
  <strong>Topics Discussed:</strong>
  <ul>
    <li>Academic and research opportunities in mathematics</li>
    <li>Careers in data science, finance, teaching, and industry</li>
    <li>Preparing for competitive exams and higher studies</li>
    <li>Mathematical curiosity and lifelong learning</li>
  </ul>
</div>
