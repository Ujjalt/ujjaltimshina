  <strong> Introduction to Nets and Filters in Topology</strong>, delivered at <strong>Ph.D. Seminar Series, Department of Mathematics, IIIT Delhi</strong>, New Delhi, India, 2024.
  
  <br><br> 
  <strong>Overview:</strong> The talk explored the generalization of sequences through <strong>nets</strong> and <strong>filters</strong>, and their role in capturing convergence and continuity in topological spaces where sequential notions are insufficient.
  
  <br><br>
  <strong>Topics Covered:</strong>
  <ul style="margin-top: 5px;">
    <li>Motivation behind nets and filters</li>
    <li>Directed sets and their role in topology</li>
    <li>Convergence via nets and filters</li>
    <li>Applications in characterizing compactness and continuity</li>
  </ul>
  
  This seminar was aimed at the general audiance".
